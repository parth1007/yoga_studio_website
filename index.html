About Us

Shree Yoga Vijnana Kendra ® is dedicated to imparting and promoting the scientific practice of yoga for the betterment of health among all segments of society, by adopting an integrated approach towards leading a joyful life.

Shree Yoga Vijnana Kendra (SYVK) is a registered charitable Trust that was established in 2013 by a group of qualified and well-experienced Yoga professionals. The noble cause of "Health and Happiness to All" has been tirelessly pursued by SYVK since its inception. Today, with more than 10 centers, SYVK has taught the ancient philosophy of Yoga in a very scientific manner to over 10,000 students and successfully trained more than 250 Yoga teachers. As an Institute of choice for Yoga aspirants in North Bengaluru, SYVK is rooted in traditional Indian culture and values while also embracing the advent of technology. With its popular online modules and courses, SYVK has reached out to aspirants not only from all parts of the city and country but also from other countries like the USA, UK, and Sweden.

Shree Yoga Vijnana Kendra (SYVK) is an affiliate of Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana (VYASA), which is located in Jigani, Bengaluru, India.
